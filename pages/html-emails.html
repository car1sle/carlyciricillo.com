I got my start in engineering through email development. I'm a pro at responsive email markup, but my specialty is email automation — At Wunderkind, I use JavaScript, Golang, Salesforce AMPscript and Responsys RPL to integrate user-personalized email content from our clients' ecommerce platforms and email service providers.

My plan for this page is to showcase my full HTML email portfolio, but for now please head to my Really Good Emails profile! You can learn more about each email example by cliking the View email in the bottom right of the view modal, then going to the Info tab.

For the last two years at Wunderkind, my quarterly goals have been contributing to Wunderkind's proprietary internal email builder tool, a React app that will allow nondevs to easily build campaigns.
