This page will talk about lists.cm, a tool I built that helps take the headache out of coding bulleted lists in HTML emails. I modeled it after Campaign Monitor's email button and background generators. I admittedly haven't worked on it in a while, and have been sitting on a big list of improvements. My dislcaimer for now is: This site won't generate a bulletproof list that you can just drop into any template — but it's a great jumping off point for experienced email developers. 🙂
